About Me

My name is Lan Ejiri and I am developer with a web development certification a Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Engineering from Rutgers Engineering.

Right after obtaining my degree, I worked as a Draftswoman at a small woodwoorking tool manufacturing company. I gained many valuable work experiences from working there for a year and a half, such at learning how to get along with coworkers and getting used to demanding work flow. However, it got stale and I signed up for a web development bootcamp, hoping to expand my skill set. After a few months, I landed a job as a product engineer at Lightspeed Voice where I work currently.
